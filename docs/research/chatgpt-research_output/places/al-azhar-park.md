# Al-Azhar Park

Arabic name: **حديقة الأزهر**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `al-azhar-park`

## Historical summary

A contemporary landscape project by the Aga Khan Trust for Culture on a former refuse mound beside the historic city. The project combined park construction with neighborhood conservation and social programs.

## Architecture summary

Terraced gardens, axial paths, planted slopes, water features, and framed views connect a contemporary park to the historic urban and cemetery landscapes around it.
