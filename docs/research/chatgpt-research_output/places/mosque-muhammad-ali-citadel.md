# Mosque of Muhammad Ali

Arabic name: **جامع محمد علي باشا**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `mosque-muhammad-ali-citadel`

## Historical summary

Built for Muhammad Ali Pasha within the Citadel between 1830 and 1848. Its design announced the ruler's modernizing authority through a conspicuous Ottoman-inspired imperial form.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A large central dome is supported by semi-domes and flanked by slender pencil-like minarets. The interior uses marble revetment, painted decoration, galleries, and a prominent courtyard.
