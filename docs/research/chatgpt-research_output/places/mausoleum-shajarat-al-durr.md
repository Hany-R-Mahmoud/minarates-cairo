# Mausoleum of Shajarat al-Durr

Arabic name: **ضريح شجر الدر**  
Status: **PROPOSED_V4**  
Slug: `mausoleum-shajarat-al-durr`

## Historical summary

Associated with Shajarat al-Durr, the powerful thirteenth-century ruler and patron who helped mark the transition from Ayyubid to Mamluk rule. The foundation is among Cairo's early Mamluk funerary monuments.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A domed funerary structure whose chamber, transition zone, and exterior mass participate in the developing Cairene mausoleum tradition.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:
- domed chamber
- muqarnas transition
- inscriptions
- funerary enclosure
