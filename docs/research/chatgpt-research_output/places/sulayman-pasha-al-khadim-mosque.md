# Mosque of Sulayman Pasha al-Khadim

Arabic name: **جامع سليمان باشا الخادم**  
Status: **PROPOSED_V4**  
Slug: `sulayman-pasha-al-khadim-mosque`

## Historical summary

Built by Sulayman Pasha al-Khadim in the Citadel during the first Ottoman period in Egypt. It is commonly identified as the first Ottoman-style mosque built in Cairo.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A domed prayer hall with Ottoman spatial organization, a surrounding cemetery and service spaces, and a minaret that differs from the dominant Mamluk forms of the Citadel.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.
