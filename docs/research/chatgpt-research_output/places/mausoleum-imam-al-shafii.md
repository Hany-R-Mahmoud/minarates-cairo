# Mausoleum of Imam al-Shafi'i

## Historical summary

The Ayyubid sultan al-Kamil commissioned a mausoleum over the tomb of Imam al-Shafi'i in 1211. The celebrated wooden dome and later fabric reflect centuries of devotion and repair.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A domed funerary chamber with a monumental wooden dome, cenotaph, mihrab, and later additions. The complex anchors a major cemetery landscape and pilgrimage tradition.
