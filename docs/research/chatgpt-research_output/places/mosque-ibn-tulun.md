# Mosque of Ahmad Ibn Tulun

Arabic name: **جامع أحمد بن طولون**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `mosque-ibn-tulun`

## Historical summary

Founded by Ahmad ibn Tulun for his new administrative city al-Qata'i. It is one of the oldest and largest surviving Islamic monuments in Cairo and preserves a major early Islamic architectural language.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A vast open courtyard is surrounded by four arcades; the outer ziyada separates the mosque from the city. Brick construction, pointed arches, stucco ornament, and the spiral minaret define the monument.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.
