# Al-Azhar Mosque

Arabic name: **الجامع الأزهر**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `al-azhar-mosque`

## Historical summary

Founded soon after the Fatimid foundation of Cairo by Jawhar al-Siqilli and completed in the reign of al-Mu'izz. It developed into one of the Islamic world's major centers of Sunni learning after later institutional changes.

## Architecture summary

A layered complex whose present fabric combines Fatimid remains with substantial medieval and Ottoman additions. Courtyards, prayer halls, riwaqs, portals, and later minarets record its long institutional life.
