# Gayer-Anderson Museum

Arabic name: **متحف جاير أندرسون**  
Status: **PROPOSED_V4**

## Historical summary

Formed from two connected historic houses beside Ibn Tulun Mosque and associated with the British officer and collector Gayer-Anderson. The museum presents domestic architecture and a historicized collection.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

The houses contain courtyards, qa'as, mashrabiyyas, service areas, painted ceilings, and carefully controlled views between public, family, and service zones.
