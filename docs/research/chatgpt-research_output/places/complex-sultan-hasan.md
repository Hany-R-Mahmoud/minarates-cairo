# Mosque and Madrasa of Sultan Hasan

Arabic name: **جامع ومدرسة السلطان حسن**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `complex-sultan-hasan`

## Historical summary

Commissioned by Sultan al-Nasir Hasan and built during a period of political turbulence and plague. The foundation was intended to teach the four Sunni legal schools and to serve as a royal funerary monument.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A monumental four-iwan madrasa organized around a deep courtyard, with tall stone walls, a domed mausoleum chamber, monumental entrance, and exceptionally large spatial scale.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:
- four iwans
- central courtyard
- domed mausoleum
- muqarnas portal
- madrasa cells
