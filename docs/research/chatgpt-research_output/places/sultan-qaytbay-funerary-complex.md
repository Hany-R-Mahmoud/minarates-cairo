# Funerary Complex of Sultan Qaytbay

Arabic name: **مجموعة السلطان قايتباي الجنائزية**  
Status: **PROPOSED_V4**  
Slug: `sultan-qaytbay-funerary-complex`

## Historical summary

Founded by Sultan al-Ashraf Qaytbay in the Northern Cemetery. The complex expresses late Mamluk patronage through a mosque-madrasa, mausoleum, sabil, and associated domestic and service structures.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A compact ensemble built around a domed mausoleum, with a ribbed stone dome, elegant minaret, prayer and teaching spaces, sabil, and related urban dependencies.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:
- ribbed stone dome
- minaret
- mosque-madrasa
- sabil
- cemetery setting
