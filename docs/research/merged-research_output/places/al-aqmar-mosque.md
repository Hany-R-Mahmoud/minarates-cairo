## Historical summary

Built in 1125 AD (519 AH) under Fatimid Vizier Al-Ma'mun al-Bata'ihi during the reign of Caliph Al-Amir bi-Ahkam Allah. Notable for being one of the earliest mosques in Cairo to feature a street-adjusted facade offset from its interior qibla orientation.

بُني عام 1125 م (519 هـ) في عهد الوزير الفاطمي المأمون البطائحي والخليفة الآمر بأحكام الله. يتميز بكونه من أوائل المساجد في القاهرة التي ضبطت واجهتها مع خط الشارع بخلاف اتجاه القبلة الداخلي.

## Architecture summary

Features an elaborately carved stone facade with stalactite hood (muqarnas) niches, a central medallion, and early Arabic epigraphy. The interior contains a small hypostyle courtyard surrounded by pointed arches.

يتميز بواجهة حجرية منحوتة ببراعة تحوي مقرنصات وميدالية مركزية وكتابات خطية كوفية. يتكون الداخل من صحن صغير مكشوف تحيط به أروقة ذات عقود مدببة.

# Al-Aqmar Mosque

Arabic name: **الجامع الأقمر**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `al-aqmar-mosque`

## Historical summary

Built in 1125 under the Fatimid caliph al-Amir bi-Ahkam Allah through the vizier al-Ma'mun al-Bata'ihi. Its street-facing stone facade responds to the alignment of the existing urban route.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A compact congregational mosque with a richly carved stone facade, a hypostyle prayer space, and a qibla-oriented interior adapted to a non-orthogonal urban site. Its facade is a major surviving Fatimid contribution to Cairo's street architecture.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

- hypostyle prayer space
- inscription bands

## Preserved gemini research note

# Al-Aqmar Mosque (جامع الأقمر)
**Status:** PUBLISHED_MEDIA  
**District:** Al-Gamaleya / Al-Mu'izz Street  
**Type:** Mosque  

## Historical Summary
Built in 1125 AD (519 AH) under Fatimid Vizier Al-Ma'mun al-Bata'ihi during the reign of Caliph Al-Amir bi-Ahkam Allah. Notable for being one of the earliest mosques in Cairo to feature a street-adjusted facade offset from its interior qibla orientation.

*بُني عام 1125 م (519 هـ) في عهد الوزير الفاطمي المأمون البطائحي والخليفة الآمر بأحكام الله. يتميز بكونه من أوائل المساجد في القاهرة التي ضبطت واجهتها مع خط الشارع بخلاف اتجاه القبلة الداخلي.*

## Architectural Details
Features an elaborately carved stone facade with stalactite hood (muqarnas) niches, a central medallion, and early Arabic epigraphy. The interior contains a small hypostyle courtyard surrounded by pointed arches.

### Key Elements
- Muqarnas Hoods
- Stone Facade Alignment
- Keel Arches
- Kufic Inscriptions

## Stories & Heritage Significance
### Street Orientation Innovation (ابتكار التوافق مع الشارع)
**Classification:** fact
Architects aligned the facade with the existing Fatimid avenue grid while rotating the interior layout toward Mecca.

## Visitor Information
- **Opening Hours:** 09:00 - 17:00
- **Ticket Price:** Included in Al-Mu'izz street visitor pass / standard historic site entry
- **Photography Rules:** Allowed without flash; tripod permissions required
- **Dress Code:** Modest dress required; head covering for women upon entry to prayer hall
- **Accessibility:** Level entrance with minor threshold step
