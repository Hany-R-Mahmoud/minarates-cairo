# Al-Azhar Park (حديقة الأزهر)
**Status:** PUBLISHED_MEDIA  
**District:** Al-Darb al-Ahmar  
**Type:** Urban Park / Landscape Heritage  

## Historical Summary
Gifted to Cairo by the Aga Khan Trust for Culture (AKTC) and opened in 2005. The park construction led to major archaeological excavations along the adjacent 12th-century Ayyubid city wall.

*أنشئت بتمويل من أغاخان للثقافة وافتتحت عام 2005. كشف مشروع الحديقة عن أجزاء واسعة من السور الأيوبي التاريخي.*

## Architectural Details
Contemporary landscape design inspired by traditional Islamic gardens, featuring geometric water channels, native flora, and terraced viewpoints overlooking Islamic Cairo.

### Key Elements
- Water Channels
- Terraced Viewpoints
- Ayyubid Wall Integration

## Stories & Heritage Significance
### Urban Transformation (التحول الحضري)
**Classification:** fact
Transformed a 500-year-old rubbish dump into a major green lung and heritage axis for Cairo.
