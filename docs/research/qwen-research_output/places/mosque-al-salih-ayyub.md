# Madrasa and Mausoleum of al-Salih Ayyub

Arabic name: **مدرسة وضريح الصالح نجم الدين أيوب**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `mosque-al-salih-ayyub`

## Historical summary

Founded by the Ayyubid sultan al-Salih Najm al-Din Ayyub. Its location on Bayn al-Qasrayn and its later Mamluk associations place it at the heart of Cairo's dynastic and educational landscape.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A street-facing madrasa with a domed mausoleum and a minaret; the complex uses a dense urban frontage and a clear separation between teaching and funerary functions.
