# Al-Rifa'i Mosque

Arabic name: **مسجد الرفاعي**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `al-rifai-mosque`

## Historical summary

Commissioned in the nineteenth century by members of the Egyptian ruling family and completed in the early twentieth century. It became a dynastic mausoleum as well as a mosque.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A monumental revival design with a large prayer hall, domed and vaulted spaces, richly ornamented surfaces, and mausoleum chambers. The complex faces Sultan Hasan across the square.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:
- prayer hall
- domes
- mausoleum chambers
- ornamental stone and stucco
