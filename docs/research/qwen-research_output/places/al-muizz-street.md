# Al-Muizz Street

Arabic name: **شارع المعز لدين الله الفاطمي**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `al-muizz-street`

## Historical summary

The principal ceremonial and commercial spine of Fatimid Cairo, named for the caliph al-Mu'izz li-Din Allah. Its surviving ensemble accumulated through Ayyubid, Mamluk, Ottoman, and modern interventions.

## Architecture summary

A linear urban sequence rather than a single building, combining portals, religious foundations, markets, houses, sabils, and wakalas. Street walls, projecting facades, and layered thresholds shape the pedestrian experience.
