# Mosque of al-Mu'ayyad

Arabic name: **جامع المؤيد شيخ**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `mosque-al-muayyad`

## Historical summary

Founded by Sultan al-Mu'ayyad Shaykh beside Bab Zuweila. The foundation was tied to the ruler's imprisonment at the site before his accession and transformed the gate into a ceremonial vertical landmark.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A hypostyle mosque with a courtyard, domed mausoleum, richly carved portal, and two minarets built above Bab Zuweila's towers.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:

- twin minarets
- monumental portal
- madrasa
