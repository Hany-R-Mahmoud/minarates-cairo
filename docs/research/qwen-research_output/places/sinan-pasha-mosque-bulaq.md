# Sinan Pasha Mosque

Arabic name: **جامع سنان باشا ببولاق**  
Status: **PROPOSED_V4**

## Historical summary

Built in Bulaq in the Ottoman period for Sinan Pasha, governor of Egypt. Its location reflects Bulaq's role as Cairo's river port and commercial gateway.
