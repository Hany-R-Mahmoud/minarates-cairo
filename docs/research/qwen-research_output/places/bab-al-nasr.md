# Bab al-Nasr

Arabic name: **باب النصر**  
Status: **PUBLISHED_MEDIA**  
Slug: `bab-al-nasr`

## Historical summary

Constructed in the late eleventh century as part of Badr al-Jamali's rebuilding of Cairo's walls. The gate's name, Gate of Victory, expresses the political and military symbolism of the Fatimid capital.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

## Architecture summary

A monumental rectangular gate framed by massive square towers and decorated with military emblems, including shield-like forms associated with the gate's identity.

يتطلب النص العربي الكامل مراجعة محرر متخصص قبل النشر العام.

Architectural elements:
- square towers
- relief shields
- vaulted passage
- fortification wall
